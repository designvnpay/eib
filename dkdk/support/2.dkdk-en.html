ARTICLE 3. AUTHENTICATION OF CUSTOMERS AND ONLINE TRANSACTIONS

  • 1. Authentication of customers upon Online banking access

    Customers accessing Online banking service shall be authenticated by their Username and Log-in password which:

    • a) The Username provided by Eximbank to customers must have the length of at least 6 characters which are not coincident or in alphabet or numberic order;

    • b) The password supplied by Eximbank should be changed right at the first access to Online banking service with the length of at least 8 characters, including numbers and letters, uppercase and lowercase, or special characters;

    • c) Password is valid up to 180 days, after which it should be changed. By such, when customers log in Online banking service, the system will check and request customers for change of password if applicable.

    • d) In case customers input wrong password for more than the allowed number of times (5 times), their Online banking service shall be locked, and customers shall request for unlocking/re-issuance of password at Eximbank Business units.

  • 2. Authentication of Online banking transactions:

    • a) By SMS OTP

      • (i) Length of SMS OTP: 6 characters.

      • (ii) SMS OTP is valid in 5 minutes from the time it is generated and sent to customers’ mobile phone number, and expired if wrongly input or out of the specified time.

      • (iii) Such form of authentication applies to individual customers and institutional customers on Internet Banking/Mobile Banking.

    • b) By PIN PKI Token

      • (i) Customers connect Token to their PC and input PIN PKI Token for authentication of transactions on Internet Banking.

      • (ii) If wrongly input for more than the allowed number of times (5 times), PIN PKI Token shall be locked, and customers shall request for its unlocking/re-issuance at Eximbank Business units.

      • (iii) Such form of authentication applies to individual customers and institutional customers on Internet Banking.

    • c) By Enhanced Soft OTP (Eximbank Smart OTP):

      • (i) This form of authentication applies to account transactions of individual and institutional customers on Internet Banking and Mobile Banking.

      • (ii) Customers shall download Eximbank Smart OTP application to their registered mobile phone/tablet to generate advanced Soft OTP for authentication of transactions.

    • d) By eKYC customer identification solution:

      • (i) This form of authentication enables to authenticate intrabank and interbank money transfer transactions performed on current accounts of Individual customers applied for opening at Eximbank via eKYC solution.

      • (ii) Individual customers shall download Eximbank Mobile Banking application to their mobile phone for use of eKYC solution.
