Provisions on “Premature principal repayment”/ “Loan settlement” applicable to individual customers making online transactions on Eximbank EDigi app

  1. “Premature principal repayment”: applied to the entire loan of individual customers at Eximbank which are eligible for online premature repayment of principal in accordance with Eximbank regulations from time to time. Accordingly, customers/their loans shall not fall under one of the following circumstances:
    1. Customers are having principal or interest due (pending demand) from loans at Eximbank (having due/overdue debts not yet paid);
    2. Customers incur debts categorized to groups 2-5 at Eximbank;
    3. Loan interest is calculated based on original principal balance (add-on);

    4. The loan is subject to debt rescheduling, exemption or reduction of interest, fees, or retention of debt category as support to customers in accordance with regulations of the State Bank and Eximbank (as per Circular 01/2020/TT-NHNN dated 13/03/2020).

      The exclusions of customers/loans specified in this clause shall not apply to loans pledged by online term deposits.

  1. “Loan settlement”: applied to loans pledged by online term deposits.
  2. Principal amount in “Premature principal repayment” transactions: at least VND2 million each and up to the remaining principal balance of the loan. Also, customers are required to pay fee for such premature debt repayment respective to the principal amount early repaid (if any) as agreed by customers and Eximbank in the credit agreement; and transaction fee (if any) as regulated from time to time.
  3. In case of premature principal repayment on disbursement date: one-day interest shall be collected in the premature debt repayment fee field.
  4. Time for conducting and recognizing “Premature principal repayment”/”Loan settlement”: 7:30 a.m. to 17:00 p.m. from Monday to Friday, except Saturday, Sunday, national and Tet holidays.
  5. Fund sources for “Premature principal repayment”/“Loan settlement”: customers’ current account at Eximbank, on which credit balance should be ensured to cover the total transaction expenses.
  6. “Premature principal repayment” plan: the principal amount early paid shall be deducted out of the final terms of the principal repayment schedule. Following payment terms shall be made upon agreement between customers and Eximbank.
  7. Coordination in handling upon customers’ completion of “Loan settlement” transactions: customers shall contact the Eximbank unit managing the loan for assistance.
  8. Customers agree that any access to Eximbank’s online banking system – Eximbank EDigi on the Internet and/or mobile phone to conduct “Premature principal repayment”/“Loan settlement” transactions must be made by customers’ log-in name and password in combination with the authentication method provided by Eximbank. Customers shall be fully responsible for such transactions and bound by all obligations, risks, and losses arisen in connection therewith, including but not limited to fraud, cheating, missing, loss, abuse, disclosure and misuse, and security vulnerability to the log-in name and/or password at customers’ fault.
  9. By use of Eximbank EDigi and the authentication method registered with Eximbank to conduct “Premature principal repayment”/“Loan settlement” transactions, customers acknowledge to have been provided by Eximbank with sufficient information relating thereto, including without limitation of information about lending interest rate, early debt repayment fee, charges applicable to the loan and other relevant information. Customers thoroughly understand and agree with all regulations and guidelines of Eximbank with regard to “Premature principal repayment”/“Loan settlement” transactions on Eximbank EDigi.
  10. Upon confirmation of “Premature principal repayment”/“Loan settlement” transactions, customers undertake to comply and are responsible for complying with regulations of Eximbank and law.
  11. Eximbank is entitled to reject any transactions if there are any abnormalities found in Eximbank Edigi account or current account of customers.
  12. For failure in “Premature principal repayment”/“Loan settlement”, customers shall contact the Eximbank unit managing the loan for specific advice.
